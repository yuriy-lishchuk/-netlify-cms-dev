By David Cohen

The [winners](https://shortyawards.com/5th-socialgood/winners/) of the [Fifth Annual Shorty Social Good Awards](https://www.adweek.com/digital/here-are-the-finalists-for-the-fifth-annual-shorty-social-good-awards/) were saluted Thursday night during an invite-only virtual event hosted by actor [Arturo Castro](https://www.adweek.com/agencies/with-witty-new-ads-massage-envy-wants-pampering-to-be-a-more-accessible-part-of-life/).

 \[caption id="" align="aligncenter" width="309"\]![](https://www.adweek.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/11/ShortySocialGoodAwardsArturoCastro.jpg) Arturo Castro. The Shorty Awards\[/caption\]

Highlights will be shared on the [Shorty Awards site](https://shortyawards.com/) following the event.

The 2020 Impact Honorees are [Gary Vaynerchuk](https://www.adweek.com/agencyspy/gary-vee-catches-heat-for-bleak-job-posting/166424/) and Adam Waheed.

\[caption id="attachment\_1230896" align="aligncenter" width="301"\]![](https://www.adweek.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/11/ShortySocialGoodAwardsGaryVaynerchuk-1024x1024.jpg) Gary Vaynerchuk. The Shorty Awards\[/caption\]

\[caption id="attachment\_1230894" align="aligncenter" width="297"\]![](https://www.adweek.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/11/ShortySocialGoodAwardsAdamWaheed-864x1024.jpg) Adam Waheed. The Shorty Awards\[/caption\]

Vaynerchuk is being recognized for a 12-hour TikTok “streamathon” that raised more than $2 million for charities, while Waheed is being called out for helping to build The Adam Waheed School in Bali, as well his work on the frontlines of the protests earlier this year over the racial divide in the U.S.
